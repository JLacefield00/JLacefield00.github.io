Welcome to Nuclear Weapons are Fake

Are nuclear weapons real? Or are they an elaborate myth born out of fear, politics, and power? At Nuclear Weapons Are Fake, we explore these questions and more, diving deep into the stories, science, and history that have shaped one of the most controversial topics of our time. This is a space for thinkers, skeptics, and truth-seekers—a platform where mainstream narratives are re-examined, and unconventional perspectives are brought to light. Whether you're here out of curiosity, doubt, or a desire to challenge what you've been told, you’ve come to the right place.

What You'll Discover

The History: How did the idea of nuclear weapons become a cornerstone of global politics? The Science: Examining the technical claims—are they all they seem? The Narrative: Who benefits from the belief in nuclear weapons, and why?

Join us in peeling back the layers of a global story that’s been accepted without question for far too long. Together, we’ll uncover the truths—or myths—that lie beneath. Start your journey today. Because the truth deserves a closer look.
